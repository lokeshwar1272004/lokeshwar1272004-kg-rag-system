Technology Companies: Key Facts and Relationships

OpenAI
OpenAI is an AI safety and research company founded in December 2015. The founding team
included Sam Altman, Elon Musk, Greg Brockman, Ilya Sutskever, Wojciech Zaremba, and
John Schulman. OpenAI is headquartered in San Francisco, California. Sam Altman serves
as the Chief Executive Officer of OpenAI. OpenAI created ChatGPT, GPT-4, DALL-E, and
the Codex programming model. Microsoft invested approximately $10 billion in OpenAI and
partnered with the company to integrate AI into Azure cloud services.

Tesla
Tesla Inc. is an electric vehicle and clean energy company. Elon Musk is the CEO and
a co-founder of Tesla. Tesla is headquartered in Austin, Texas. The company produces
electric cars including the Model S, Model 3, Model X, Model Y, and Cybertruck.
Tesla also manufactures energy storage products and solar panels. JB Straubel
co-founded Tesla and served as Chief Technical Officer.

SpaceX
Space Exploration Technologies Corp (SpaceX) is a private aerospace manufacturer.
Elon Musk founded SpaceX in 2002 and serves as its CEO and chief engineer. SpaceX is
headquartered in Hawthorne, California. The company developed the Falcon 9, Falcon Heavy,
and Starship rockets. SpaceX created the Starlink satellite internet constellation.

Google and Alphabet
Google was founded by Larry Page and Sergey Brin in 1998 while they were PhD students
at Stanford University. Sundar Pichai is the CEO of Google and of its parent company
Alphabet Inc. Google is headquartered in Mountain View, California. Google developed
TensorFlow, the BERT language model, and the Gemini AI system. Google owns YouTube,
which was acquired in 2006. Google Cloud competes with Amazon AWS and Microsoft Azure.

Microsoft
Microsoft Corporation was founded by Bill Gates and Paul Allen in 1975. Satya Nadella
has served as CEO of Microsoft since 2014. Microsoft is headquartered in Redmond,
Washington. The company acquired LinkedIn in 2016 for $26 billion and GitHub in 2018
for $7.5 billion. Microsoft Azure is a major cloud computing platform. Microsoft
partnered with OpenAI and integrated GPT models into its products.

Meta Platforms
Meta Platforms, formerly known as Facebook, was founded by Mark Zuckerberg at Harvard
University in 2004. Mark Zuckerberg is the CEO of Meta. Meta is headquartered in
Menlo Park, California. Meta owns Instagram (acquired 2012), WhatsApp (acquired 2014),
and the Oculus VR headset brand. Meta developed the LLaMA language model series and
PyTorch, a popular deep learning framework.

Amazon
Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994 in Seattle, Washington. Andy Jassy became
CEO of Amazon in 2021. Amazon Web Services (AWS) is the company's cloud computing
division and is a market leader in cloud infrastructure. Amazon acquired Whole Foods
Market in 2017 and MGM Studios in 2022. Amazon developed the Alexa voice assistant.
